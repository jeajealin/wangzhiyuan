Artworks > 2000 > Fragments

M.D.F Acrylic，Multimedia Installation view Dimensions variable
 
 

This artwork was created when I graduated from the Academy of Fine Arts of Sydney in the year 2000. It is made of 40 different object shapes. There is no logical connection between them. My idea is: suspect its originality. So, these 40 images are copied from others or from what I made. I just designed the side and color and re-organized them. Each image does not have a fixed position in the exhibition, and the title does not have any specific meaning, it is just called 'Fragments'.
