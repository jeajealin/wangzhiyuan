Artworks > 2022 > I know the darkness（1-10）

Charcoal on paper, 225x152cm/each
 
 

7）

我躲着并且害怕你，看着懒洋洋的阳光，挥不去的如同前面的路，虽然伸手不见五指。雨水不断的下，要习惯于这无聊的午后，你这个笨蛋。逃避？一个致命的平庸年代已经到来。

Hiding and afraid of you and looking at the lazy sun, what I can’t shake off is like the road ahead even though I can’t see the fingers of my hand. The rain keeps falling so just get used to the uneventful afternoon, stupid. A fatal era of mediocrity has already arrived.
