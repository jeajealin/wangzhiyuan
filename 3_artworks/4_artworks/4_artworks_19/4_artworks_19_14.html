Artworks > 2004-2005 > They

Bronze In total:200 1000x500cm
 
 

This installation artwork 'They' consists of 200 bronze sculptures and wooden boxes. The gallery provides the broad visual space for 'They,' as it creates the silence and pressure that church does. All the audience needs to do is to look up and watch the 200 small solemn bronze 'They' uniformed installation works, increase the 'silence' for audiences with a sense of awe but also their oppressive mood. And at the same time, the audience turns from being the spectator to being the spectacle.
